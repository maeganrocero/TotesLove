About me

Hello! Thanks for stopping by! Here you can purchase hand painted canvas tote bags! When I was young, I was always drawing. As a hobby, I would paint on canvases, tote bags, and iPhone cases whenever I had free time. I would post the on social media and many people asked if they could purchase one. I have been thinking about it for a while and I decided to make one of my dreams come true. Making one of my hobbies into a business!

My goal is to create a self-hosted website for my business.
